WHO WE ARE

Galaxy Home Care is a company, which will be providing non-medical, in-home support services and assisted living placement services to people wanting to live independently in their own homes and communities in the state of Pennsylvania. Our Market research shows a strong need for quality home care services within this region and we are convinced that by employing competent, well-educated and highly staff, we can become the Home Care Agency of choice in the state of Pennsylvania.

Our service model is based on responsive individualized care tailored to the specific needs of our clients. We work towards offering a wide range of services to help cater to the needs and demands of individuals in need of residential services and their families.

Our Mission

The mission of Galaxy Home Care is to help our client’s lead dignified, independent lifestyles in the comfort and safety of their own homes by carefully assessing and fully understanding their individual needs and selectively placing trained personnel to meet these needs according to state Regulations. In turn Galaxy Home Care will provide staff with competitive compensation, an inviting work environment with knowledgeable an innovative management.
